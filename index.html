About

Maggie started learning piano at the early age of 6. Piano has been her life. She graduated from the Tianjian Conservatory of Music. Throughout her education she also taught piano to younger students. Her education encompasses not only performances but also the teaching aspects of piano. After graduating, Maggie has since performed at multiple events such as the Beijing Hotel and and other concert performance.

From China, she brings her skills to the United States. Maggie taught at the La Canada School of Music. She also teaches privately. Piano is Maggie’s passion. She hopes to bring her music to you and share it with you. She wants to make your occasion something to remember. No matter the event, she will bring professionalism and a love of the music to fulfill your wishes.

At Your Service

Maggie can provide a wide range of piano services for small and large venues alike. For any occasion or event, piano music by Maggie will bring that extra special touch. Whether you are looking for a private pianist for dinner music or pianist for wedding or an accompanist, Maggie can provide that service. Maggie can also be a part of an ensemble should you need a spot filled. Finally, Maggie is a long time teacher. One-on-one lessons are available in studio or she can travel to your location.
